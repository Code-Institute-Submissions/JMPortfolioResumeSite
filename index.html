about

Chapter 1: after graduating from Business and Finance in DCU I have found myself in a variety of roles that had elements of process enhancement, liaising with Tech teams and trying to find solutions to IT problems - this is what kept me in financial services and also where I have gotten little tastes for my actual passion. In US Bank I was a supervisor in a centralised operations team where automation was our primary focus. I worked with VBA, ETL Tools, SQL tables and started to develop my interest in coding. In JP Morgan I was an AVP working on a transformation project where we were tasked with building back office software using an outsourced vendor. By identifying business needs, clients’ needs & working with our vendor we built a robust solution from start to finish. Since moving back to Wexford and joining Carne group I have found myself being able to focus on what parts of my career I have enjoyed the most and digging through ETL rules, creating small resource tools through Excel VBA and Automating processes is what stood out.

Chapter 2: begins with progressing through Code Intitutes Full-Stack Software Development Program. I am very excited about my new venture leading me to a career in software development and I look forward to the interesting projects and opportunities that may present themselves as a result.
